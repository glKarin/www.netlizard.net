Gold Solitaires-2
Video Capability Buy

Sequel to the most famous and loved by millions of people five Windows patiences.
All sorts of special effects, amazing graphics, animation, learning course and lots of other innovations await for you in this unforgettable game!

Classic “Klondike”, “FreeСell”, “Spider”, “Pyramid”, “Monte-Carlo” are included in this game. We are sure you all know and enjoy playing them…!

These patiences will become the most favorite pastime for young students and adults!

“Klondike” – you need to build up four foundations by suit from Ace to King. And the tableau piles can be built down by alternate colours, and partial or complete piles can be moved if they are built down by alternate colors also. Any empty piles can be filled with a King or a pile of cards with a King at the top.

“FreeСell” – you need to move all cards to foundations using open cells as intermediate cells. To win you need to build up four foundations by suit from Ace to King.

“Spider” – The main purpose of this game is to remove all cards from the tableau, assembling them in the tableau before removing them. Initially, all cards are dealt to the tableau in 7 piles, face down except for the top cards. The tableau piles build down by rank, and in-suit sequences can be moved together. When a pile from King to Ace is generated it automatically moves to the foundation.

“Pyramid” – your goal is to remove from pyramid all the cards for which the sum total of points amounts to 13.

“Monte-Carlo” – you need to free the playing field, removing cards of same value, which are placed next to each other horizontally or cornerwise.
